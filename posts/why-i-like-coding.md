## Why I Like Coding

Coding is a way to turn ideas into working products. It helps me solve problems, learn continuously, and collaborate with great people.

- It is creative problem solving.
- It enables automation of repetitive tasks.
- It allows building software that impacts users.

I enjoy the sense of accomplishment from shipping and improving code incrementally.
